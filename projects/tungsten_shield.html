Tungsten Shield

High-density shielding component for NDT imaging

Overview

Designed a tungsten shielding component to improve image quality and reduce scatter for NDT scenarios. (Specific details omitted due to classification constraints.)

Build Process

  1. Requirements: Defined attenuation, thickness, and mounting constraints.
  2. Design: Parametric CAD with filleted edges and alignment features.
  3. Manufacturing: Material selection, tolerances, and surface finish considerations.
  4. Assembly: Fastener spec; thermal expansion and stress checks.
  5. Validation: Bench evaluation with representative sources/detectors (high-level only).
  6. Documentation: Drawings, BOM, and handling/storage guidance.

Results

  • Improved signal-to-noise in targeted imaging configurations.
  • Robust mount compatible with existing fixtures.
  • Design and fabrication package prepared for controlled distribution.
