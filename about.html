Who I Am

I build robust, real-world robotics—bridging autonomy, embedded systems, and perception. My recent work includes integrating a Compton camera with a Boston Dynamics Spot for autonomous gamma-source localization (Gamma Seeker) and deploying inspection robots in industrial environments.

Outside the lab, I'm usually lifting at the gym or getting in weekend runs. I'm also a gamer (currently on Battlefield 6), a live-music junkie, and a serial trip-planner. I love hiking, camping, and chasing stamps in my National Park passport—ideally with friends and a bit of spontaneous adventure.
